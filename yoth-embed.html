[ 01 — THE CONCEPT ]

Why the Horse.

Speed, independence, forward momentum, bold action. In the Chinese zodiac, the Horse is not passive or cautious — it moves first. For a 2026 swimwear launch built around competitive swimming, that makes it more than a zodiac graphic. It becomes a symbol of mindset.

[ THE STRONGEST IDEA ]

No one carries you forward.

When the buzzer sounds, the athlete is alone in the lane. The result comes from their own preparation, nerve, and willingness to move. That is exactly what the Horse represents — self-propulsion, and the instinct to go forward without waiting for the field.

[ THE CONNECTION ]

Attack the distance.

Competitive swimming is judged by movement, acceleration, and the ability to commit fully to a fixed distance. The best swimmers are decisive — explosive off the blocks, holding speed under pressure, pushing through resistance to the wall. That is the Horse in motion.

[ THE DROP ]

A symbol, not just a print.

For 2026, the Horse gives this collection meaning beyond the design. It is a statement of competitive mindset — bold action, freedom of movement, and the drive to go first. Produced through FINIS, it carries both identity and the credibility of a genuine brand partnership.

[ 02 — CAMPAIGN VISUALS ]

On the body. At the pool.

The Year of the Horse collection is built to stand out on the pool deck — bold graphics, sharp identity, and the energy of the Horse on every piece. You're not just wearing a design. You're wearing a signal: galloping forward, moving with speed and determination, and making it clear before the race even starts.
